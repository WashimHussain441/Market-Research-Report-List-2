<p><p>The High-Intensity Focused Ultrasound (HIFU) aesthetics market is primarily categorized into two types: ultrasound-guided and MR-guided. Ultrasound-guided HIFU uses real-time imaging to precisely target facial and skin tissues for tightening and rejuvenation, enhancing treatment accuracy. In contrast, MR-guided HIFU utilizes magnetic resonance imaging to provide detailed anatomical insights, improving the localization of treatments for deeper penetration and effectiveness. Both types focus on non-invasive procedures, offering effective anti-aging solutions with minimal recovery time.</p></p>
