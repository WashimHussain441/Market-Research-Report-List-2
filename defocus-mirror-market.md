<p><p>The defocus mirror market is categorized into two primary types: peripheral defocus and multipoint defocus. Peripheral defocus involves mirrors designed to create a blurred effect at the edges of the visual field, enhancing depth perception and improving visual comfort in various applications, such as display technologies. Multipoint defocus, on the other hand, generates multiple defocused points across the visual field, allowing for a more immersive experience in virtual reality and advanced imaging systems, enhancing user engagement and realism.</p></p>
