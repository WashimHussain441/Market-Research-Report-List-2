<p><ul><li>Press Molding</li><li>Casting Molding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Quartz Kitchen Countertops market encompasses two primary manufacturing types: press molding and casting molding. Press molding involves compacting quartz particles mixed with resin under high pressure, creating a dense, durable surface ideal for countertops. In contrast, casting molding involves pouring a resin-quartz mixture into a mold, allowing for intricate designs and finishes. These methods cater to diverse consumer preferences, with press molding emphasizing strength and uniformity, while casting molding offers flexibility in shapes and aesthetics.</p></p>
