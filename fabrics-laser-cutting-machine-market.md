<p><p>The fabrics laser cutting machine market is primarily segmented into three types: CO2 laser cutting machines, fiber laser cutting machines, and others. CO2 laser cutting machines utilize carbon dioxide gas to generate a laser beam, which is effective for cutting various fabrics, especially non-metal materials. Fiber laser cutting machines use a solid-state laser with a fiber optic cable, offering high precision and speed, particularly for complex designs. "Others" may include hybrid or specialized machines catering to niche applications in the textile industry.</p></p>
