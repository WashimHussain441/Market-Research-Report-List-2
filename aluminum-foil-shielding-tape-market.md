<p><p>The Aluminum Foil Shielding Tape market comprises two main types: one-sided and double-sided. One-sided aluminum foil tape features adhesive on a single side, primarily used for electrical insulation and EMI/RFI shielding applications. In contrast, double-sided aluminum foil tape has adhesive on both sides, making it ideal for applications requiring secure bonding and sealing. Both types serve critical roles in industries such as electronics, automotive, and construction, providing effective shielding against electromagnetic interference while ensuring durability and conductivity.</p></p>
