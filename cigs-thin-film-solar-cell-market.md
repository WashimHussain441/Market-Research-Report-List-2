<p><p>The CIGS thin film solar cell market primarily consists of two types: TF PV (thin-film photovoltaic) technology and traditional PV technology. TF PV technology utilizes a lightweight and flexible film composed of copper, indium, gallium, and selenium, offering advantages like efficiency in low-light conditions and versatile applications. In contrast, traditional PV technology typically involves crystalline silicon panels, known for higher efficiency and durability but being less flexible. Both markets cater to different applications and consumer needs, driving innovation and growth in solar energy solutions.</p></p>
