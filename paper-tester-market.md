<p><ul><li>Physical Tester</li><li>Optical Tester</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The paper tester market is categorized into two main types: physical testers and optical testers. Physical testers evaluate the mechanical properties of paper, including tensile strength, tearing resistance, and burst strength, providing insights into durability and usability. In contrast, optical testers assess the visual characteristics of paper, such as brightness, opacity, and color consistency, which are crucial for print quality and visual appeal. Together, these testing types ensure paper products meet industry standards and customer expectations.</p></p>
