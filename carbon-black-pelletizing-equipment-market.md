<p><p>The Carbon Black Pelletizing Equipment market consists of two primary types: wet pelletizing and dry pelletizing. Wet pelletizing involves the addition of liquid binders to carbon black particles, enhancing cohesion and producing denser pellets, which are ideal for specific applications. In contrast, dry pelletizing relies on mechanical pressure alone to form pellets without moisture, resulting in suitable products for various industrial uses. Both methods cater to differing production needs and end-user requirements in the carbon black industry.</p></p>
