<p><p>The screen printing glass market is categorized into two primary types: manual and mechanized. Conversely, mechanized screen printing employs automated machines for high-volume production, ensuring consistency and efficiency. Mechanized systems are ideal for large-scale operations, where speed and precision are needed, while manual methods cater to niche markets and unique designs, providing a balance between creativity and efficiency.</p></p>
