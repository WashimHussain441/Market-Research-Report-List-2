<p><ul><li>Beam Block</li><li>Beam Trap</li><li>Charged-Particle Beam Dump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The beam dump market encompasses three main types: beam blocks, beam traps, and charged-particle beam dumps. Beam blocks are designed to absorb the energy of high-intensity particle beams, preventing damage to surrounding equipment. Beam traps serve a similar function but are more specialized, often incorporating materials that efficiently convert beam energy into heat. Charged-particle beam dumps are focused on absorbing particles like electrons or protons, ensuring safety and efficiency in experimental and industrial applications by mitigating radiation hazards.</p></p>
