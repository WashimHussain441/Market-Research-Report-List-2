<p><p>Benchtop FTIR (Fourier Transform Infrared) Spectrometers are categorized into two main market types: AC (Alternating Current) and DC (Direct Current). AC FTIR spectrometers utilize modulated light sources and are known for their high sensitivity and ability to analyze dynamic samples. In contrast, DC FTIR spectrometers employ continuous light sources, providing stable and consistent measurements, making them suitable for applications requiring reliable data over prolonged periods. Both types cater to diverse industries, including pharmaceuticals, environmental monitoring, and material science.</p></p>
