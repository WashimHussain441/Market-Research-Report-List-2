<p><p>The fluorosilicone gum market is categorized into two primary types: high temperature vulcanization (HTV) and room temperature vulcanization (RTV). HTV fluorosilicone gums are designed for applications requiring durability under extreme heat, making them ideal for aerospace and automotive sectors. In contrast, RTV fluorosilicone gums cure at room temperature and are favored for general sealing and coating applications where ease of use and quick curing are essential. Both types offer excellent chemical resistance and flexibility, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
