<p><p>The graphene powder market comprises three primary types: monolayer flakes, multilayer flakes, and nanoplatelets. Monolayer flakes consist of a single layer of graphene, offering exceptional electrical and thermal conductivity, making them highly sought after for advanced electronics and coatings. Multilayer flakes contain multiple graphene layers, providing a balance of properties for applications in composites and energy storage. Nanoplatelets are thicker than monolayers, featuring a more extensive surface area, ideal for enhancing mechanical strength in various materials.</p></p>
