<p><p>The Dry Etching Machine market encompasses three primary types: Conductor Etching, Dielectric Etching, and Polysilicon Etching. Conductor Etching is focused on patterning conductive materials like metals for semiconductor devices. Dielectric Etching targets insulator materials, crucial for forming isolation layers. Polysilicon Etching involves the removal of polysilicon layers, essential in photovoltaic cells and integrated circuits. Each segment serves distinct applications in the semiconductor and electronics industries, driving innovations in device miniaturization and performance enhancement.</p></p>
