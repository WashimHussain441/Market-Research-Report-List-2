<p><p>The wound protector market comprises two main types: plastic and metal wound protectors. Plastic wound protectors are lightweight, flexible, and typically single-use, designed to prevent contamination during surgical procedures. They are favored for their affordability and ease of disposal. In contrast, metal wound protectors are durable and reusable, offering enhanced stability and protection against external contaminants. They are preferred for complex surgeries where prolonged use is necessary. Both types aim to improve patient safety and surgical outcomes by safeguarding surgical sites.</p></p>
