<p><ul><li>ABS Material</li><li>PC Material</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The snow helmet market is primarily categorized into two types based on material: ABS (Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene) and PC (Polycarbonate). ABS helmets are known for their sturdiness and impact resistance, making them suitable for casual snowboarders and skiers. In contrast, PC helmets are lighter and offer enhanced durability and flexibility, appealing to both professional and performance-oriented users. Each material serves specific user needs, affecting the helmets' weight, protection levels, and overall comfort during winter sports activities.</p></p>
