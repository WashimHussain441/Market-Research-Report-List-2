<p><ul><li>Direct Mechanical Transmission</li><li>Hydraulic Transmission</li><li>Pneumatic Actuator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wave power generation equipment utilizes ocean waves to produce energy through various transmission methods. Direct mechanical transmission involves converting wave motion into electricity using mechanical systems like gearboxes and turbines. Hydraulic transmission uses the movement of waves to pressurize hydraulic fluids, which drive turbines for energy generation. Pneumatic actuators harness wave energy by compressing air within chambers, converting the energy into mechanical work that generates power. Each type offers unique advantages in efficiency and application for wave energy systems.</p></p>
